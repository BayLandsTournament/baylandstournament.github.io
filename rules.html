💰 Entry Fee & Prize

Entry is $20 per team.

1st Place Prize is $200 then 2nd Place Prize is 100$ and 3rd is $75.

For example, if 32 teams join, the prize could reach $400 or more.

👥 Teams & Format

The tournament is 2v2 (two players per team).

Each team signs up you and a friend.

No goalkeepers are allowed because the goals will be small.

The field will be small — perfect size for fast 2v2 games.

📅 Date & Time

The tournament will take place on May 16.

2:00 PM – 5:00 PM.

⏱ Game Time

Each match lasts 12 minutes.

6 minutes per half.

⚽ Ball Out of Play

When the ball goes out, play restarts with a kick-in (throw-in with your feet).

The opposing team must give at least 5 feet of space.

🚫 Fouls & Safety

Any very violent or extremely dangerous foul results in automatic disqualification.

A small perimeter around the goals will be marked.

Players are not allowed to enter this area to prevent someone from standing in front of the goal.
